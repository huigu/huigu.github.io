The Critique of “A FREE RECTANGULAR PLATE ON ELASTIC FOUNDATION”

    First, this paper was written by Cheng Xiang-sheng. The author came from Tongji University in Shanghai.
And it was published in Applied Mathematics and Mechanics (English Edition, Vol. 13, No. 10, Oct. 1992) .

    This article discussed the bending problems of the rectangular plates with free boundaries on elastic foundations.And the author selected a fiexural function which satisfies not only all the geometric boundary conditions on free edges wholly but also the boundary conditions of the total internal forces. We apply the variational method meanwhile and then obtain better approximate solutions.
